About the Lab

Welcome to the AI & Control Research Laboratory. Our research focuses on the intersection of Control Theory, Machine Learning, and Robotics.


We aim to develop algorithms that allow autonomous systems to operate safely and efficiently in uncertain and dynamic environments. Our current work spans model-based reinforcement learning, adaptive control, and safety-critical AI.

Hardware

Real-world validation on quadrotors and robotic arms.
